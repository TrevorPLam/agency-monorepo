# f3-apps-client-sites-brand-foundation: Constraints

## Hard constraints

- Keep brand logic app-local until the two-surface threshold is met.
- Keep client brand packages client-scoped, not generic.
- Do not let brand packages absorb content, analytics, SEO, or business logic.
- Keep naming explicit so cross-client reuse is never implied accidentally.

## Documentation constraints

- Keep allowed and forbidden contents explicit.
- Keep the package conditional in all references.
- Align extraction language with `e10-apps-client-sites-foundation`.
