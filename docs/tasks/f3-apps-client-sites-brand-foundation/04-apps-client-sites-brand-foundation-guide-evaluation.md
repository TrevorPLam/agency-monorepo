# f3-apps-client-sites-brand-foundation: Guide

## When to use this task family

Use this family when a client already has more than one surface and you need to decide whether brand tokens should stay local or move into a client-scoped package.

## Evaluation workflow

1. Identify the real client-owned surfaces in play.
2. Confirm the brand system is non-trivial and genuinely shared.
3. Separate tokens and reusable overrides from content and business logic.
4. Keep the package client-scoped if extraction is justified.

## Review questions

- Are there at least two real surfaces now?
- Is the shared material truly brand infrastructure rather than page or workflow logic?
- Would extraction reduce duplication without widening blast radius?
